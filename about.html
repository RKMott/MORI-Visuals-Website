What is MORI About?

I'm based in Bedford, right in the heart of the UK, where I split my time between gritty backstreets and dimly lit music venues — camera in hand, caffeine on standby. This isn’t just a hobby or a side hustle; it’s a daily pursuit to freeze those raw, blink-and-you-miss-it moments that make street life and live music so electric. Whether it’s the glint of neon off rain-soaked pavement or the sweat and chaos of a dive bar gig, I chase what feels real — unfiltered, unscripted, and unapologetically alive.



Photography, for me, is about doing justice to the details most people scroll past. I’m drawn to textures, movement, the clash between light and shadow — all the things that make a scene feel like it’s breathing. When I shoot, I’m not interested in perfect smiles or staged poses. I’m there to catch the mood, the mess, the energy — because that’s where the story lives.



This whole journey kicked off when I realized how many incredible artists, moments, and cultures were being flattened into forgettable snapshots. Since then, I’ve been on a mission to create something that hits harder — images that feel like the moment, not just record it. If you're in a band that wants to be shot with the same intensity you bring to the stage, or you're looking for visuals that actually say something, we’ll probably get on well.



So whether it’s a midnight walk through the city or a basement gig that shakes the floor, I’ll be there — documenting it with a sharp eye and a deep respect for the stories behind every frame.

Freqently Asked Questions

What types of photography do you specialise in?

I focus on capturing the raw energy and real moments across live music, street photography, portraits, events, and lifestyle shoots. Whether it’s a packed venue, a quiet city corner, or a personal session, I aim to tell stories through every frame. However, this doesn't stop me from reaching out into other areas, whether that be shooting a music video or promotional shots for movie bill boards.

Do you travel for shoots?

Absolutely. While I’m based in Bedford, I’m more than happy to travel for the right project — whether it's across town or across the country. Let’s talk details and make it happen.

Can we collaborate on a creative concept?

Yes, 100%. I’m all about bringing ideas to life. If you’ve got a mood, story, or aesthetic in mind, I’ll help shape it into something that looks and feels true to you.

How do I book a session?

Drop me a message through the contact page or reach out via socials. Let me know what you’re after, and I’ll get back to you with availability, pricing, and a plan to make it work.

How long does it take to get the photos back?

Turnaround is usually 1–2 weeks, depending on the project size. I always make sure the final edits are worth the wait — no rushed jobs, just solid work.
