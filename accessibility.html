Accessibility Policy

For MORI

Effective Date: [Insert Launch Date]

At MORI, we are committed to ensuring that our website and services are accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. We believe in equal opportunity and access, and we strive to provide a user-friendly experience for all clients, regardless of ability or technology.

Commitment to Accessibility

MORI is actively working to meet the standards set by the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, and comply with relevant legislation such as the UK Equality Act 2010.

We aim to ensure that all visitors can:

  • Navigate our website using a keyboard or screen reader
  • Access all content with sufficient colour contrast
  • Read and understand text clearly, including through alternative text for all meaningful images
  • Experience smooth performance across modern web browsers and devices

Image Accessibility

As a photography-based service, we understand the importance of making our visual content accessible. We provide alt-text descriptions for images where appropriate, especially where images convey information or context beyond aesthetics.

Contact for Accessibility Support

If you experience any difficulty accessing any part of our website, or if you have a suggestion for how we can improve accessibility, we encourage you to get in touch.

Accessibility Support Email: [Insert business email upon launch] We aim to respond within 3–5 business days.

Future Improvements

While we currently do not host video or audio content, we are planning to expand our digital offerings. When this happens, we will ensure:

  • Captions or transcripts are provided for videos
  • Audio content includes alternative formats where possible

We are continuously reviewing our content and website design to make improvements and remain compliant with evolving accessibility standards.

Feedback

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of the MORI website and services. Your suggestions help us identify areas for improvement and better serve our diverse audience.
